    Algorithm: Reorder Point (ROP)

    ROP = (Avg Daily Demand × Lead Time) + Safety Stock. Velocity computed per SKU from your data. Category-specific lead times applied. Simulated stock = 8% of total sold (prototype). XAI explanation shown per SKU.

    Algorithm: Price Elasticity of Demand (PED) + XAI

    PED = % Change in Qty ÷ % Change in Price. Each SKU gets a full XAI explanation showing the category benchmark, velocity modifier, and expected weekly revenue delta. Use the Promo Simulator tab to test specific discount scenarios.

    No data loaded
    Upload a CSV to compute PED scores and pricing recommendations.
    Algorithm: FP-Growth Market Basket Analysis

    Lift ≥ 1.2 threshold applied. Orders are first grouped by OrderID to form shopping baskets. If multi-item basket volume is low, a co-occurrence fallback ensures bundle cards always populate. Cross-PED and ideal bundle price computed per pair.

    No data loaded
    Upload a CSV with multi-item orders (same OrderID, multiple SKUs) for FP-Growth basket analysis.
    Algorithm: SARIMA (1,1,1)(1,1,0)[7] Demand Forecast

    Weekly sales are decomposed into trend, seasonality (7-week cycle), and residual. 4-week projection with 90% confidence interval. Use the forecast peak weeks to pre-position inventory before demand spikes.

    How to use the promo simulator
    1Select a product from your uploaded data in the dropdown above.
    2Enter a discount percentage (1–80%). This represents a platform promo code or markdown.
    3Click Run simulation. The system uses the SKU's PED score to predict volume uplift and revenue/profit impact.
    4If the promo is not profitable (PED too low = inelastic), try a smaller discount or use the Bundles tab to increase AOV instead.

    CSV Data Requirements
    I-ODSS auto-detects column names from Shopee, Lazada, TikTok Shop exports and any custom CSV. Column names are case-insensitive. If your file uses non-standard headers, the system will show you exactly what it found vs what it needed.

    Four columns are required. All others are optional but improve analysis quality.

    SKU / ProductName Required
    sku, product_sku, ProductName, item_name, SellerSKU, sellersku, product id…
    Date Required
    date, order_date, created_at, waktu pesanan, timestamp, tarikh…
    Quantity Required
    quantity, qty, units, unit_sold, jumlah, kuantiti…
    Price Required
    price, unit_price, selling_price, harga, UnitPrice, harga asal…
    OrderID Recommended
    orderid, order_id, order_number, no pesanan — needed for FP-Growth bundling
    Category Optional
    category, kategori — improves PED and ROP accuracy
    Region Optional
    region, state, negeri, buyer_state — for regional EDA charts
    Rating / Returns Optional
    rating, review, returns, refund — for EDA quality metrics
